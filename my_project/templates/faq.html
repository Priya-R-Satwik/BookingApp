Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I make a booking?

You can make a booking by selecting your desired date and available room.

2. What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

3. Can I change my booking?

Yes, changes can be made by contacting our support team.

4. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards and other specified payment methods.

5. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach out via the contact form on our website or email us directly.
